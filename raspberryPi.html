What is Raspberry Pi?

Raspberry Pi is a small single-board computer. It was created to help teach computer science but gained popularity because of its cost, compact size, and flexibility. It has grown in popularity. There are different types of Raspberry Pi out there, but in this class, CSE 299, we will only be focusing on the Raspberry Pi 4 Model B.
